# v1.2 Bug Ledger

**Purpose:** record confirmed issues found after the v1.1 capped review loop when applying the upgraded review protocol with a 6-round cap.

## Policy

- Confirmed P0/P1/P2 issues reset the consecutive clean count to 0 after they are fixed.
- Extreme, rare, or normal-use-non-impacting P3/minor issues may be fixed while still counting the round as clean.
- Every confirmed issue, including minor-clean issues, must record description, root cause, fix summary, affected files, verification, and status.
